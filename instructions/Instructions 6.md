# STEP 6 — CART + CHECKOUT (STYLE REVIEW FLOW)

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE

Design Cart and Checkout as a **continuation of the styling system**, not a separate ecommerce flow.

Cart should feel like:

→ reviewing decisions

→ completing something already resolved

## ⚠️ CORE RULES

- No generic ecommerce UI
- No clutter (discounts, upsells, etc.)
- No visual drop in quality

Everything must feel:

→ calm  
→ structured  
→ intentional
