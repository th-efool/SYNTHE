\\# STEP 5 — PRODUCT PAGE (DECISION ENGINE)







\\## 🎯 OBJECTIVE







Transform the product page into a \\\*\\\*decision engine\\\*\\\*, not a product listing.







The page must answer:







→ WHY does this work for me?







NOT:







→ what is this product?







\\---







\\## ⚠️ CORE RULES







\\- No generic ecommerce layout



\\- No clutter (reviews, ratings, etc.)



\\- No overwhelming detail







Everything must feel:







→ structured  



→ intentional  



→ explanatory  







\\---







\\# 🧠 1. PAGE STRUCTURE (MANDATORY)







\\---







\\## Layout:







```text



\\\[ Image Gallery ]        \\\[ Product Info ]







\\\[ Explanation Block ]







\\\[ Works Best With ]







\\\[ Complete the Look ]



Two-column layout (desktop):







LEFT:



→ image system







RIGHT:



→ information + reasoning







🖼️ 2. IMAGE GALLERY



Purpose:







Show product clearly without noise







Must include:



main image (large)



2–4 secondary images



Behavior:



click → swap main image



no zoom complexity yet



Component:







Use ImageContainer







🧩 3. PRODUCT INFO BLOCK



Must include:



Title:







→ "Soft Linen Jacket"







System Tags:







Use Tag component:







Soft Natural



Soft Autumn



Natural / Romantic



Short Descriptor:







1–2 lines:







Light structure. Natural drape. Muted warmth.



🔥 4. EXPLANATION BLOCK (CORE OF PAGE)



Title:







"Why this works"







Content:







Structured explanation:







Structure:



"Follows your natural width without adding rigidity."



Color:



"Holds warmth and softness against your skin tone."



Presence:



"Supports a relaxed, natural impression."



IMPORTANT:







Do NOT write long paragraphs.







Use:







→ short, precise lines







🧠 5. “WORKS BEST WITH” SECTION



Purpose:







Contextual compatibility







Title:







"Works best with"







Layout:



2–3 ProductCards



Content:







Items that:







complement silhouette



match color harmony



reinforce essence



🔥 6. “COMPLETE THE LOOK” (HIGH VALUE)



Purpose:







Show full outfit composition







Title:







"Complete the look"







Layout:







Use:







OutfitCard



Content:



2–4 pieces combined



visually grouped



Behavior:







Click → navigate to product







🧠 7. CTA (MINIMAL, CONTROLLED)



Add:







Single action:







Add to wardrobe →



DO NOT:



add price yet



add quantity selector



add cart clutter



🧠 8. DATA USAGE







Use:







mockProducts







For now:







hardcode relationships (worksWith, outfit)







No dynamic logic yet.







📐 9. SPACING + VISUAL RULES



follow Step 3 spacing system



clear separation between blocks



avoid large empty gaps



Hierarchy:



Image > Title > Tags > Explanation > Related



✨ 10. MOTION (SUBTLE)







Apply:







fade-in on page load



slight image transition on change







NO:







heavy animation



parallax



bouncing



🧪 11. FINAL TEST







Check:







✔ Explanation is clear



✔ Tags are visible



✔ Layout is balanced



✔ Not cluttered







🚫 DO NOT DO



No reviews



No ratings



No pricing



No recommendations engine yet



No filters



✅ SUCCESS STATE

"this explains why it works"
